Our Story

Origins
The Genesis

Forged in the heart of Nelspruit.

The Mpumalanga heat is unforgiving. We realized that investing in premium, high-performance flasks like Stanley or Yeti was only half the equation. The bottleneck was exactly that: the bottleneck.

Standard ice cubes shatter, melt instantly, and dilute the experience. What started as a localized solution for long, sweltering drives to the Kruger National Park rapidly evolved into a vision to redefine hydration entirely.

More than frozen water. A meticulously engineered lifestyle upgrade.

We are a collective of designers and hydration purists dedicated to solving the mundane frustrations of the 'On the Go' lifestyle.

Our Mandate

Bridging the 'On the Go' Lifestyle Gap.

Your life moves fast. Your hydration shouldn't hold you back. Our mission is to seamlessly integrate long-lasting, tailored coldness into your daily momentum, ensuring uncompromising refreshment wherever the journey takes you.

Zero Artificial Syrups

Frequently Asked

What are your standard delivery times?
+
We guarantee next-day delivery within our primary Nelspruit and White River zones for orders placed before 2 PM. Barberton deliveries are scheduled on Tuesdays and Fridays.
Can I adjust my ice subscription?
+
Absolutely. Our subscription model is fluid. You can pause, upgrade your flavor tiers, or modify delivery frequency by contacting our direct line at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled dispatch.
Do you accept custom flavor requests?
+
For our exclusive clientele and bulk corporate orders, we craft bespoke infusions. From specific citrus blends to proprietary herbal profiles, indicate your request in the inquiry form and our artisans will consult with you.
